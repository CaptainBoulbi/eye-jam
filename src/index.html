RESEARCH JOURNAL - DAY 45
Dr. Elena Vasquez
"The rats in Group B are exhibiting abnormal behavior. Aggression toward other subjects. Insensitivity to pain. A... disturbing appetite. I reported these anomalies to the committee. They told me to continue. “The benefits outweigh the risks.” I'm not sure I agree. Personal note: I had nightmares last night. The rats were staring at me. They were smiling.
That's ridiculous. Rats don't smile."
- E.V.


The escape of one of the test subjects from Project Lazarus has triggered a zombie apocalypse on the surface. You are the last survivor of an experiment designed to defy death. Dr. Vasquez took care to document the project. Use her notes to understand what happened more than a kilometer underground, where it all began. Will you find a vaccine to save the world, or will you watch it disappear into chaos?
